COVID-19 Update

COVID-19 has impacted our ability to complete our mission. We had some amount of money collected and were planning to utilize it this summer on our first official project.

We had been in contact with a school for kids with physical disabilities in Russia and were planning to go there and donate equipment that would help them get more involved in science and technology. We were also planning to teach them about the gadgets they were receiving, and how to use them. The goal was to expose them to something they may not have known about in the hopes that it could positively impact their future. If not for COVID-19, we would have currently been in the process of completing this endeavor. The plan is still to complete this project sometime in the future, but as of now, it is unclear when that will be possible.

Like many people, businesses, and schools have done during this unprecedented time, we too must manage our expectations on what we can achieve. The previous plan, for the foreseeable future, is unattainable, but an impact can still be made.
